THE BEGINNING

STORY OF EXPERT STYLISTS

After picking up the best stylists in town, we opened the doors to our elegant and posh men’s salon in April of 2015. Located in DHA, Gulshan-E-Iqbal, and Gulistan-E-Johar, Men’s Salon strives to deliver these areas glamour flared with an all-around city sensation.The Men’s Salon staff’s classic vision is held chief to Karachi standards and is made up of an eclectic company of trend-setting artists from around Pakistan who all contribute to a passion for timeless and contemporary beauty and grace.
